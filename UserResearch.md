# User Research

## Objective

Understand the needs of users while ordering food online.

## Research Method

- Online Survey
- Interviews
- Observation

## User Needs

- Easy Login
- Fast Search
- Quick Checkout
- Secure Payment
- Order Tracking

## Pain Points

- Slow Apps
- Complex Checkout
- Too Many Steps
- Poor Navigation

## Conclusion

Users prefer a simple and clean mobile interface with minimal steps.
